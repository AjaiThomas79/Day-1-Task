Difference between HTTP1.1 and HTTP2
1. HTTP1.1 was founded in year 1997 and HTTP2 came into existence around 2015
2. Bothe are used for communication or transfer of data between client computer and a remote web server.
3. HTTP1.1 uses plain text format for every single requests and responses whereas HTTP2 encapsulates data in binary format for requests and responses.
4. HTTP1.1 is less faster compared to HTTP2.
5. If there is network delay then using HTTP1.1 loading of pages may take longer time whereas in HTTP2 despite network delay, page will load faster due to multiplexing
